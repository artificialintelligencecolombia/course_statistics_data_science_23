---------------------
POBLACION/MUESTRA
---------------------

- Poblacion: el conjunto total de datos. (Parametros).
- Muestra: Subgrupo representativo de la poblacion, seleccionado aleatoriamente.
(Estadistico).

---------------------
DATA COLLECTION
---------------------

There are 2 types of studies according to the collection method:

a. Estudio Observacional:
- The sample is raw, not processed.
- Data is collected in unidim or bidim. tables.

b. Estudio Experimental:
- The sample is processed.
- We identify the biases of the sample and how does them affect the statistics.
- The sampling must to be completely RANDOM!
- There's a control group (blind: they dont know which one will get placebo.
2xblind: there is a separation between placebo and experimental, but they
don't know it.) and a study group.
