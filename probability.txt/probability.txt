==============================
PROBABILITY
==============================

Statistics:  branch of mathematics that involves collecting, analyzing, 
interpreting, presenting, and organizing data.

Probability: branch of mathematics that deals with the likelihood or chance of 
events occurring. 

---------------------
SIMPLE PROBABILITY 
---------------------

P(A) = evento_favorable_A / Espacio_muestral (Total de resultados posibles)

Example:
- Cual es la probabilidad que, al tirar un dado, la cara que nos muestre sea 2:

P(2) = 1 / 6

KEY POINTS: 
- Probability goes from 0 to 1.
- In a simple probability, the events must have the same probability of
    occurrence.

---------------------
EXPERIMENTAL PROBABILITY 
---------------------

P(A) = numero_veces_ocurre_A / Total_intentos

- Sebasa en la observacion y medicion del evento sobre una muestra de intentos.
- Its important to perform a considerable number of experiments for more  precise 
results.
